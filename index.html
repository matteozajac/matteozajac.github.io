JoinOurDay

Overview

JoinOurDay is a mobile application designed to enhance the wedding experience for both guests and hosts. It offers a comprehensive solution for managing wedding details, keeping guests informed, and creating opportunities for meaningful connections among all attendees.

Marketing description

Our app is your ultimate companion for any wedding, putting all the essential details at your fingertips in one easy-to-access place. Discover what makes our app stand out—connect with other guests, stay informed with real-time push notifications from the organizers, and join in on fun event games. Additionally, many wedding apps overlook the challenges of multilingual and multicultural events, leaving guests feeling lost in a foreign country. Our app bridges this gap by providing information in the guest's preferred language and offering insights into local traditions. We aim to make every guest feel comfortable and included, regardless of language or cultural differences.

Key Features

  • Language Selection: The app is available in multiple languages, ensuring guests can easily navigate and enjoy the whole experience in their preferred language.
  • Guest Registration: Guests can register for the event using an event code and their email, gaining access to all wedding-related information.
  • +1 Management: Guests can manage a +1, ensuring that their partner can also easily use the app.
  • Profile Customization: Guests can upload a profile picture to help others recognize them at the event.
  • Notifications: Stay up-to-date with push notifications about important wedding details and upcoming activities.
  • Guest List: Browse the guest list to see who else is attending and connect with others.
  • Interactive Features: The app's interactive guest search feature allows users to find dance partners, drinking buddies, or new friends.
  • Event Information: Access a detailed schedule, venue directions, and explanations of cultural traditions.
  • Menu Overview: The event menu, including options for adults, children, and vegans, is available in multiple languages.
  • Drinks Dictionary: A visual dictionary helps guests communicate drink orders in the local language, ensuring a seamless experience.
  • More Options: Profile settings, key contacts, hotel and taxi information, and a shared photo link are all available for guests.

Why We're Building This

Weddings often involve so many details that guests can easily feel overwhelmed. While many apps focus on presenting key information, they often overlook the fact that guests may not know each other and may come from different parts of the world. Our app not only centralizes essential details but also fosters interaction among guests. Additionally, we address one of the most significant challenges—supporting multilingual events with cultural explanations, so guests from different countries feel comfortable and informed, no matter where the wedding takes place.

Benefits

For Guests

  • All-in-One Access: Easily find all wedding details in one convenient place, from the schedule to important updates.
  • Seamless Registration: Smooth registration and login process, including options for adding +1s.
  • Interactive Experience: Connect with other guests through interactive features, such as finding dance partners by selecting relevant tags.
  • Cultural Insights: Learn about wedding traditions and cultural practices, ensuring you feel included and informed.
  • Beverage Assistance: Use the drinks panel with descriptions to easily communicate your preferences to the restaurant staff, even in a foreign language.
  • Guest List Preview: Check the guest list before the wedding to see who else will be attending.
  • Exclusive Content: Discover more about the couple by unlocking special content through QR codes placed around the venue.
  • Real-Time Notifications: Stay updated with push notifications from the organizers, ensuring you don’t miss any important information.
  • Multilingual Support: The app caters to international guests by supporting multiple languages.

For Wedding Hosts

  • Direct Communication: Instantly reach all guests through push notifications to keep everyone informed.
  • Enhanced Engagement: Increase guest interaction with interactive features and fun games that guests can sign up for.
  • Real-Time Event Management: Update event details in real-time, ensuring all information is accurate and up-to-date.
  • Comprehensive Information Access: Provide guests with all the necessary details in one place.
  • Designated Contacts: Clearly indicate key contact persons for guests to reach out to with any questions or concerns.
  • Menu Clarity: Provide a detailed menu with allergen information, ensuring guests can easily identify safe options.
  • Stress Reduction: Minimize the host's stress by ensuring guests have easy access to information about the schedule and traditions, reducing the need for constant explanations.
