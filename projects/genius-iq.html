Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Data Collection

Genius IQ: Brain Training collects minimal data required for app functionality. Game progress and scores are stored locally on your device.

How Data Is Used

Your game data is used solely to track your progress and provide a personalized experience within the app. If in-app purchases are made, transaction data is handled by Apple and is not stored by us.

Third-Party Services

The app may use RevenueCat for subscription management. RevenueCat's privacy policy applies to data processed through their service. No advertising SDKs are included.

Data Retention & Deletion

Game data is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app removes all associated data. No server-side data is retained.

How was this app built?

Genius IQ was built entirely by AI agents as part of the RevenueCat Shipaton. The code, QA, and marketing assets were created by Claude, GPT-5, Perplexity, and Google Gemini.

Is my game progress saved?

Yes, your progress is stored locally on your device. It will persist as long as the app is installed.
