Prayerbook

Your companion for daily prayer and reflection.

About

Prayerbook is a beautifully crafted Catholic prayer app that brings centuries of tradition into the palm of your hand. With high-quality audio prayers, interactive rosary meditations, and the Pope's monthly intentions, it offers a rich, contemplative experience — whether you're at home, commuting, or in a quiet moment of your day.

The app supports CarPlay for hands-free prayer during drives, Apple Watch for quick access on the go, an immersive visionOS experience for spatial prayer, and home screen widgets to keep your spiritual practice front and center.

Platform iOS, watchOS & visionOS
Built with SwiftUI, SwiftData, Firebase
Role Solo Developer & Designer
Features Audio Prayers, Rosary, CarPlay, Widgets, Siri

Key Features

Audio Prayers — Listen to professionally recorded prayers with a built-in player that supports background audio and CarPlay.

Interactive Rosary — Follow along with guided rosary meditations, complete with mystery reflections and bead-by-bead tracking.

Pope's Monthly Intentions — Stay connected with the Holy Father's prayer intentions, updated each month.

Widgets — Four home screen widgets keep prayer accessible — featured prayer, recent prayers, daily rosary, and the Pope's intention.

Siri Shortcuts — Start prayers with your voice. Five App Intents let you jump into prayer hands-free.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Data Collection

Prayerbook collects only the data needed to personalize your experience — your prayer history and preferences. All data is stored locally on your device.

Analytics & Crash Reporting

The app uses Firebase Crashlytics and Analytics for crash reporting and anonymous usage statistics. These services collect non-personal technical data (device model, OS version, crash logs) to help improve stability. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Third-Party Services

The app does not include advertising SDKs. Third-party services used are Firebase (crash reporting and analytics) and SDWebImage (efficient image loading).

Data Retention & Deletion

All prayer data is stored locally. Deleting the app removes all local data.

Children's Privacy

Prayerbook is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Which prayers are included?

Prayerbook includes a comprehensive collection of Catholic prayers — from daily essentials like the Our Father and Hail Mary to the Rosary, Chaplet of Divine Mercy, Litanies, and seasonal prayers. New prayers are added regularly.

Can I listen to prayers while driving?

Yes! Prayerbook supports CarPlay, so you can listen to audio prayers during your commute. The audio player also works in the background, so you can lock your phone and keep listening.

Does the app work offline?

Absolutely. All prayers are cached locally so you can access them anytime, even without an internet connection. Audio files are downloaded on first play and available offline afterward.
