Spatial Computing Experiments

Exploring what's possible beyond the screen.

About

A collection of prototypes and experiments exploring spatial computing paradigms on Apple Vision Pro and visionOS. From portal-based level selection to immersive photo experiences, these projects push the boundaries of how we interact with digital content in physical space.

Highlights include experimenting with visionOS Portals for 3D content windows, the ImagePresentationComponent for navigable spatial photos, and Liquid Glass design explorations since WWDC25.

Platform visionOS
Built with SwiftUI, RealityKit
Role Explorer & Prototyper
Status Ongoing experiments

visionOS Portals

Liquid Glass Design
