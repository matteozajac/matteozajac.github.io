Speak New York

Practical NYC English for people learning the city in real time.

About

Speak New York is a subway-inspired English learning app designed for new arrivals who need to sound natural fast. The experience focuses on movement through the city, social fluency, and work-life confidence instead of abstract classroom vocabulary.

The app combines structured lesson lines, translator support, and local context so learners can move from survival English to neighborhood-level confidence.

Platform iOS and Flutter
Purchases RevenueCat with App Store billing
Backend Firebase Auth, Firestore, Storage, Analytics, Crashlytics
Status App Store launch setup in progress

Launch Model

Speak New York launches with three one-time lesson packs and one all-access subscription. Packs are grouped by real user jobs: getting around the city, understanding culture and plans, and feeling more natural at work.

Speak New York Pro unlocks every pack while active, removes translator limits, and gives access to future pack releases during the membership period.
