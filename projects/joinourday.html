Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Data Collection

JoinOurDay collects only the data you provide directly within the app, including wedding event details, guest lists, and planning preferences. We do not collect personally identifiable information beyond what is required for app functionality.

How Data Is Used

Your data is used solely to provide the wedding planning features within the app. Data is synced across your devices using Apple's CloudKit service and is stored in your private iCloud container.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs are included in the app.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is stored in your personal iCloud account. You can delete all app data at any time by removing the app from your device and clearing the associated iCloud data through Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí iCloud ‚Üí Manage Storage.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync my data across devices?

JoinOurDay uses Apple's CloudKit to automatically sync your data across all devices signed into the same iCloud account. Make sure iCloud is enabled in your device settings.

Can I share planning access with my partner?

Yes! JoinOurDay supports shared planning through CloudKit sharing. Invite your partner from within the app to collaborate on your wedding plans.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete all app data by removing the app and clearing the associated iCloud data through your device's Settings app under iCloud ‚Üí Manage Storage.
