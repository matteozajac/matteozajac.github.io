Global Tap 26

A premium 16-bit retro soccer juggling challenge for iOS.

About

Global Tap 26 is a fast arcade game about keeping a soccer ball in the air for as long as possible. Pick a country, tap with precision, chase higher combos, and climb Game Center leaderboards.

The app is built as a paid game. There are no ads, no subscriptions, and no extra purchases. Unlocks come from gameplay coins, daily rewards, and skill.

Platform iOS
Model Paid app, play-to-unlock
Services Game Center and Firebase
Status App Store launch setup in progress

Game Loop

The goal is simple: tap the ball before it drops. Gravity ramps up, wind and vuvuzela blasts add pressure, and streaks trigger retro audiovisual rewards.

Coins earned through play unlock country balls, trails, pitch skins, and power-ups. Every run is short enough for a quick break, but score chasing gives the game long-term progression.
