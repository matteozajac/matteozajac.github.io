Privacy Policy

How Global Tap 26 handles game progress, rankings, and support data.

Overview

Last updated: June 2026

Global Tap 26 stores gameplay progress so the app can remember your high score, selected country, coins, unlocked cosmetics, settings, and daily reward state. Most of that information is stored on your device.

Data We Store

Global Tap 26 stores your scores, selected country, unlocks, coins, and settings locally using device storage. You can remove local game data by deleting the app from your device.

If you choose to use Game Center, Apple handles your Game Center identity, achievements, and leaderboard participation under Apple's own privacy practices.

Country Rankings

Global Tap 26 uses Firebase Firestore to maintain aggregate country-vs-country tap totals. The app submits the selected country identifier and the number of taps from completed runs so the global country ranking can update.

The country ranking is designed around aggregated totals, not personal profiles.
