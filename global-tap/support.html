Gameplay Progress

Scores, coins, unlocks, settings, and selected country are stored on your device. If you reinstall or delete the app, local progress may be removed by the operating system.

If gameplay feels wrong after an update, restart the app first and then contact support with your device model, iOS version, and what happened.

Game Center

Leaderboards and achievements require Game Center. If scores do not appear, confirm that Game Center is enabled on the device and that the device has a network connection.
